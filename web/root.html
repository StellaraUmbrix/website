About Me

I'm Robyn-Dawn (Robyn for short), a computer scientist, software engineer and writer from England (United Kingdom). I am currently a student with plans of studying Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence at university, with further plans to graduate as a doctorate in the field of CS and AI. I am also currently learning German, however, I am not fluent in it yet.
My hobbies include programming, writing and gaming. I am currently interested in the SCP Foundation Wiki, and EAS Systems including Alert Ready and NZ Civil Defence. I am also interested in the field of Artificial Intelligence, and the benefical uses of it towards humanity as a whole.
I am using my writing skills to write EAS Scenarios for YouTube and a future Scenario based project (which I will announce when I have more information). With that, I am learning video editing and video production to bring these scenarios to life. These scenarios can be found on my YouTube channel The World's Break
which is a channel dedicated to EAS Scenarios and other EAS related content. The project is fully free and open source, and can be found on the bot's website.
